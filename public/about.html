ABOUT ME
Hi! I am a process-driven UX Designer based in Seattle, specialized in interaction design and product thinking. At my core I am a UX strategist who believes that highly effective product definition requires insight into the intersection of design, business, technology and the user.

I have worked as a interaction designer / project manager at Asia’s largest startup crowdfunding design agency with 12+ clients (including Fortune 100 Most Innovative company, startups and non-profits.) I draw on my product thinking experience to define product vision, craft compelling stories and design seamless, human-centered experiences to solve real-world problems.

I am pssionate about interfacing with clients and product teams to bring innovative ideas to life. I also thrive on building multidisciplinary teams, and most importantly, envisioning experiences that delight users. Currently, I am pursuing Master’s degree in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) at University of Washington.
