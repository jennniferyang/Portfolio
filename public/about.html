ABOUT ME
Hi! I am User Experience Designer based in Seattle, currently pursuing Master’s degree in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) at University of Washington.

Prier to entering design, I have been trained by Google for UX, branding and marketing skills, and worked as an Interaction Designer / PM for 2 years at a design consulting agency. I draw on my product thinking experience to envision how people experience products and create solutions that not only enhance value and efficiency, but also are delightful to use. I have proven ability to always keep business value in mind while designing for users.

Working closely with the developers and designers for website design and product improvement, I value empathy in crafting designs that speak to the audience with measurable results. Now as a designer, I am energized by taking on new problems, understanding people’s unspoken needs, and drive impact on turning ideas into engaging design solutions.
