ABOUT ME
Hi! I am User Experience Designer, currently pursuing Master’s degree in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) at University of Washington.

Prier to entering design, I have been trained by Google for digital marketing skills, and worked as a PM for 2 years at a marketing agency focusing on crowdfunding and e-commerce products. I draw on my project management and marketing background to envision how people experience products and create solutions that not only enhance value and efficiency, but also are delightful to use. I have proven ability to always keep business value in mind while designing for users.

Working closely with the developers and designers for website design and product improvement, I value empathy in crafting designs that speak to the audience with measurable results. Now as a designer, I am energized by taking on new problems, understanding people’s unspoken needs, and drive impact on business challenges and product growth with engaging design solutions.
