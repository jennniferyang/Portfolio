Animal Savior

Animal Savior is the first ever crowd-sourcing sex toy in Taiwan. Known for its ground-breaking animal façade as well as its delicate, soft texture, it came in second in “crowd sourcing” categoty of the udn Design Award in 2016. Backer-Founder assisted with the overall marketing planning for Animal Savior, achieving the one million target within 42 hours. It won 1546 supporters over 44 days, which is 350% of the target fund!


Early Phase

- Strategy Setting: More than 70% of the sex toy market has been possessed by males. However, the characteristics of “Animal Savior” gives a sense of cuteness, safe, trustworthy as well as being affordable all the while. These portraits allows us to set its target audience as females who are buying sex toys for the very first time. This concept was applied to the style of crowdfunding page design, video and promotion in order to attract and communicate with potential customers.

Campaign Production


- Copywriting
Through copywriting, we have given the product a vivid image. (1) The harmless appearance of small animals:
Animal Savior is a little gadget that makes you happy rather than an actual sex toy. It is equivalent to your makeup gadgets - fits in your purse! (2) Why would I need a sex toy?
“Just a gentle touch, which doesn’t have to be the touch of your lover - makes you feel loved and cared.” Consumers would break the impression of sex toys being sexy and steamy.
(3) Certified material and vibration:
Extremely soft medical silicone is used, providing a gentle vibration and experience.

Before the preorders start, people has been deliberately discussing which animal they wish to buy. So we have tailored an unique characteristic for each animal, along with suggestions of what kind of personalities would they fit in.

- Motion Graphic Design
Based on previous experiences, videos are the most wide-spread crowd funding campaign. We have chosen to create motion graphic from the very beginning - with colorful and coherent hues that leaves a strong visual impression.

The lives of small animal is a metaphor for body parts. The adorable illustrations elaborates the fact that this is something natural, “girls also have the right to love themselves”.

- Commercial Photography
In order to create the overall visual tone of the product, we have mapped out two sets of commercial photography:
(1) Product Scenario: Surrounding small animals and living environments, warm colors are used in order to create impressive visual styles.
(2) Accessories: Each animal corresponds to a specific type of girl, which has been put together with various accessories that appears in a girl’s purse - thus resonating with the consumer.

- Web Planning

- Warning Signs: According to law, those under 18 years of age is not allowed to enter this site or purchase any sex toys. However, we were hoping that people would take sex toys in a rather polite manner. We redesigned the pop-up window according to the current style of the website, where users would be less resistant towards warning signs. This has drastically lowered the bounce rate.
- First screen: We attracted users with full-sized product scenario photography, which successfully leads them to our website for more information.
- Page Break: Each animal has their own unique page break. This creates a specified scenario and complete background story along with the commercial photography.

After the project is on line

- Advertising
Sex toys are a sensitive product category. Facebook advertising is entirely banned. Therefore, we attempted other means in order to keep our product exposed to the target audiences:
- Google Adwords Advertising: We have bought keywords such as “Animal Savior”, “sex toys”, keeping our advertisement rank within top 3. This lures user on to our website as they are searching for related products.
- Organic Traffic from Videos: Since the Motion Graphic of Animal Savior is extremely impressive, we have gotten more than 700 shares and 20,000 views on the very first day it was published.
- YouTube adds: Motion Graphic has been spread out on YouTube at an extremely low cost. 26% of those who have watched more than 10 seconds of the video has been guided to our website to understand more about the product (while an average click rate is approximately 5%-10%).

- Social Media Promotion
Since the restrictions are quite rigid, we chose to promote via the diffusivity of social networks:
- Media Cooperative Guidance: We teamed up with PopDaily, which was an online media built for girls. PopDaily has written a blog to introduce Animal Savior according to their target audiences.
[PHOTO]: The blog style fits what the target audiences likes, therefore two blog posts has brought us 10% of the total funds.
- Blogger Samples: We target users who are “first time sex toy buyers”. The emphasis wasn’t put on bloggers who are specialized in blogging about sex toys. Instead, we have contacted makeup bloggers and fashion bloggers, stressing the assuring and adorable characteristics of “Animal Savior”. This has successfully brought in conversions from potential consumers.

Ticwatch 2

Ticwatch 2 is a neat smartwatch that raised over two million USD on Kickstarter, ranked as the second-highest smartwatch product surpassed only by Pebble, and was highly praised by renowned international medias including CNN, TechCrunch, Engadget, etc.
After completing global shipping, Ticwatch 2 launched the "Taiwan Limited Edition" which is specially customized for Taiwanese consumers. Backer-Founder assisted with the overall marketing strategies of the "Taiwan Limited Edition", and the sales number exceeded 11 million NT Dollars within the first three weeks of the pre-order campaign!

Early Phase
- Pretotyping / Fake Door Market testing
We designed three different types of questionnaires by targeting three different audiences: "people interested in wearable devices", "people interested in other smartwatch brands" and “tech-savvy” to identify a clearer consumer profile of potential consumers who are interested in Ticwatch 2.
More than 5,000 feedbacks were collected within a month, which helped us to discover the most compelling feature and buying incentives to consumers, and to clarity the marketing focus of our website and advertising.
- Email Marketing
Before the the pre-order campaign started, we sent preferential information via e-mails to potentials users to drive customer loyalty by providing exclusive offers.
By targeting accurate potential consumers, the average email open rate reached over 50% (average email open rate of other technology products: 15-25%).

Campaign Production


- Commercial Photography
We re-photographed the commercial photos for Ticwatch 2, focusing on the product itself and the most commonly used functions in life the consumers cared about.

- Website Design
We can better determine what the consumers wants to see on our website, what images and messaging truly connect with them, and how we can emphasize the product value of Ticwatch 2 by knowing “why” the users visit our website in the first place.

Therefore, we confirmed the following goals to create more engaging experiences with engineers and designers together when planning the website:
(1) A clear site flow allowing users to focus on the watch itself
- Listed the achievements on Kickstarter and international media exposure: to increase consumer confidence toward crowdfunding products
- Product features with common scenarios: Allowing users to imagine how the functions they care about can be applied to life
- Demo experience: To increase site interaction
- Purchase & Specification Details: To provide the speculations and details of the product
(2) Improving site SEO rank with accurate keywords for users to search
(3) A user-friendly and smooth checkout process
We create a better user experience by simplifying the three steps of the original checkout process into one single page.
