Animal Savior

Animal Savior is the first ever crowd-sourcing sex toy in Taiwan. Known for its ground-breaking animal façade as well as its delicate, soft texture, it came in second in “crowd sourcing” categoty of the udn Design Award in 2016. Backer-Founder assisted with the overall marketing planning for Animal Savior, achieving the one million target within 42 hours. It won 1546 supporters over 44 days, which is 350% of the target fund!


Early Phase

- Strategy Setting: More than 70% of the sex toy market has been possessed by males. However, the characteristics of “Animal Savior” gives a sense of cuteness, safe, trustworthy as well as being affordable all the while. These portraits allows us to set its target audience as females who are buying sex toys for the very first time. This concept was applied to the style of crowdfunding page design, video and promotion in order to attract and communicate with potential customers.

Campaign Production


- Copywriting
Through copywriting, we have given the product a vivid image. (1) The harmless appearance of small animals:
Animal Savior is a little gadget that makes you happy rather than an actual sex toy. It is equivalent to your makeup gadgets - fits in your purse! (2) Why would I need a sex toy?
“Just a gentle touch, which doesn’t have to be the touch of your lover - makes you feel loved and cared.” Consumers would break the impression of sex toys being sexy and steamy.
(3) Certified material and vibration:
Extremely soft medical silicone is used, providing a gentle vibration and experience.

Before the preorders start, people has been deliberately discussing which animal they wish to buy. So we have tailored an unique characteristic for each animal, along with suggestions of what kind of personalities would they fit in.

- Motion Graphic Design
Based on previous experiences, videos are the most wide-spread crowd funding campaign. We have chosen to create motion graphic from the very beginning - with colorful and coherent hues that leaves a strong visual impression.

The lives of small animal is a metaphor for body parts. The adorable illustrations elaborates the fact that this is something natural, “girls also have the right to love themselves”.

- Commercial Photography
In order to create the overall visual tone of the product, we have mapped out two sets of commercial photography:
(1) Product Scenario: Surrounding small animals and living environments, warm colors are used in order to create impressive visual styles.
(2) Accessories: Each animal corresponds to a specific type of girl, which has been put together with various accessories that appears in a girl’s purse - thus resonating with the consumer.

- Web Planning

- Warning Signs: According to law, those under 18 years of age is not allowed to enter this site or purchase any sex toys. However, we were hoping that people would take sex toys in a rather polite manner. We redesigned the pop-up window according to the current style of the website, where users would be less resistant towards warning signs. This has drastically lowered the bounce rate.
- First screen: We attracted users with full-sized product scenario photography, which successfully leads them to our website for more information.
- Page Break: Each animal has their own unique page break. This creates a specified scenario and complete background story along with the commercial photography.

After the project is on line

- Advertising
Sex toys are a sensitive product category. Facebook advertising is entirely banned. Therefore, we attempted other means in order to keep our product exposed to the target audiences:
- Google Adwords Advertising: We have bought keywords such as “Animal Savior”, “sex toys”, keeping our advertisement rank within top 3. This lures user on to our website as they are searching for related products.
- Organic Traffic from Videos: Since the Motion Graphic of Animal Savior is extremely impressive, we have gotten more than 700 shares and 20,000 views on the very first day it was published.
- YouTube adds: Motion Graphic has been spread out on YouTube at an extremely low cost. 26% of those who have watched more than 10 seconds of the video has been guided to our website to understand more about the product (while an average click rate is approximately 5%-10%).

- Social Media Promotion
Since the restrictions are quite rigid, we chose to promote via the diffusivity of social networks:
- Media Cooperative Guidance: We teamed up with PopDaily, which was an online media built for girls. PopDaily has written a blog to introduce Animal Savior according to their target audiences.
[PHOTO]: The blog style fits what the target audiences likes, therefore two blog posts has brought us 10% of the total funds.
- Blogger Samples: We target users who are “first time sex toy buyers”. The emphasis wasn’t put on bloggers who are specialized in blogging about sex toys. Instead, we have contacted makeup bloggers and fashion bloggers, stressing the assuring and adorable characteristics of “Animal Savior”. This has successfully brought in conversions from potential consumers.

Ticwatch 2

Early Phase

Pretotyping / Fake Door Market testing

We designed three different types of questionnaires by targeting three different audience groups: (1) people interested in wearable devices, (2) people interested in other smartwatch brands, and (3) tech-savvy consumers, to identify a clearer potential consumer profile.

More than 4,000 responses were collected within a month, which helped us identify the most compelling features of and buying incentives to consumers, and to further clarity the focus of our website and advertising.

Email Marketing

Before launching the pre-order campaign, we sent preferential information via e-mail to potentials users to drive customer loyalty by providing exclusive offers.

By targeting accurate potential consumers, the average email open rate reached over 45% (average email open rate of other technology products: 15-25%).

Campaign Production Phase

Commercial Photography

We re-photographed commercial photos for Ticwatch 2, focusing on the product itself and the most commonly used functions catering to consumers’ needs.

Website Design

Ticwatch Taiwan Official Website: www.ticwatch2.com.tw We wanted to know why users who were interested in Ticwatch 2 visited our website in the first place, so, we confirmed the following goals to create more engaging experiences with engineers and designers together when designing the website:

(1) A clear site flow allowing users to focus on the product itself Listed the achievements on Kickstarter and international media exposure to boost consumer confidence toward crowdfunding products Product features with common scenarios: allowing users to see how the functions they care about can be applied in real life Demo experience to increase site interaction Purchase & specification details: ensuring users have all the necessary information
(2) Improving site SEO ranking with accurate keywords for users to search
(3) An intuitive, user-friendly checkout process
We also improved user experience by simplifying the three steps of the original checkout process into one single page.

After The Project Is Launched

Media Promotion

The target users of media promotion are journalists. Therefore our goal was to create attractive news points to increase their press value.
Our press releases focused on three major topics in our press kit as listed below, and successfully attracted over twenty mainstream media reports, including Apple Daily (the largest online media in Taiwan) and Yahoo! 3C. Successful crowdfunding record: Ticwatch 2 ranks as the second-best smartwatch on Kickstarter The first and only customized version: "Taiwan Limited Edition" is the first customized version internationally Product highlights: the most complete cross-platform (Android / iOS) smartwatch at an extremely affordable price

Press Conference

To enhance exposure, we also held a press conference on the first day of the pre-order campaign, providing journalists with sufficient news materials including product demos and the introduction of the development team, meanwhile boosting consumers’ confidence.

We invited the development team of Ticwatch 2 on the press conference to unveil the “Taiwan Limited Edition” first time in public.

Social Media Promotion

Since Taiwanese consumers are more skeptical towards crowdfunding products, especially high-priced products such as smartwatches, we believed abundant and reliable user reviews played a vital role in consumer’s purchase decision. Therefore we invited the top 20 domestic 3C bloggers to try out Ticwatch 2 and to share product details and experience on their social media pages. In addition, we also communicated with the development team based on the first-hand feedback of these early-adapters to adjust and optimize the operating system of “Taiwan Limited Edition”

We found that 20% of Ticwatch 2's pre-order amount was brought in by the bloggers’ reviews (60% was from advertising, 10% from media reports, and the rest from native searches), proving that reliable user reviews are trust-worthy references for consumers and can drive conversion.

Advertising

Most of our advertising costs were spent on Facebook. 5% of the sales numbers were used for advertising, and the conversion brought by ads accounted for 60% of the total sales. We continued to monitor ad performance and used different images, emphasizing different functions to conduct A / B testing for the same set of audiences to understand users’ true preferences, and what the best-performing ad images were. We continued to optimize the ads based on these results.

・By evaluating CTR (Click Through Rate), we could learn whether the ads were appealing enough for users to click the link.
・We also calculated the conversion rate through the tracking code such as Google UTM. On the first week of the pre-order campaign, the conversion rate (ROI) of the ads reached an amazing 1: 130; the data remained steadily at 1:10 or even higher a month later, proving that we were effectively advertising to the accurate audience and providing correct and appealing content.
